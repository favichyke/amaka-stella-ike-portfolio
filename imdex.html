Case Study 01 — Revenue Analytics
Stellar Team Revenue Analytics

Case Study 02 — Team Performance
Stellar Team Performance Dashboard

Problem: Team of 5 reps had no performance benchmarks, no leaderboard, and no accountability structure. Top performers were carrying underperformers with no visibility.

Solution & Action

Built a team performance tracking system with individual KPIs, deal closure metrics, and a live leaderboard. Introduced daily activity tracking and weekly coaching sessions for bottom performers.

Result Achieved
1,560
Deals Closed — Team Activity up 5.4% — 7,300 Calls/Week — Individual Revenue up to ₦7.2M per rep
Case Study 03 — Sales Overview
Stellar Team Sales Overview

Problem: No unified sales overview across all reps. Conversion rates varied wildly between team members (16%–22%) with no system to identify why top performers were succeeding.

Solution & Action

Created a comprehensive sales overview system with individual conversion rates, revenue per rep, lead counts, and a top sales leaderboard. Used data to replicate top performer strategies across the team.

Result Achieved
22%
Peak Conversion Rate — ₦23.8M Total Revenue — 860 New Leads — Top Rep: ₦7.2M Revenue Generated
